Brief Biography

Hello, my name is Kaydee Kelly. I am 24 years old and I was born on July 15th 1998. I graduated highschool in 2017 with honors in arts. I attended Southwest Tennesee Community from 2017 to 2019, but due to covid I didn't graduate. I'm currently attending code school through the Collective Blueprint and Code School to become a web developer. While I enjoy coding and web development, character design is my true passion. In my free time I work on my creative writing skills. With all of these skills combined, I aspire to become a solo indie RPG game developer.

I currently work as a freelance character concept artist as well as an event manager for the Art Coven, a small collective of novice to intermetdiate artists who uplift small fan and roleplay communties by delivering quality character art. In addition to a creative career choice, I am currently an aspiring streamer and author.

My pleasure is work and work is my pleasure. Most of my free time has its wires crossed into my work. I enjoy art, writing, and roleplay. I'm not a large fan of uncreative hobbies, with the exception of competitive gaming.
